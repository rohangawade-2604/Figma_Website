About Zia Wellness

Welcome to Zia Skin + Wellness, a medical aesthetic and holistically centered wellness practice located in the beautiful town of Sedona, Arizona! Our medical aesthetic offerings include neurotoxin (wrinkle reducing agents such as a Botox, Xeomin and others), dermal fillers for facial enhancement and volume replacement (such as Juvederm, Sculptra, Restylene and others), and PRP (platelet rich plasma) therapies for targeted innate healing in many areas of the body. We take a holistic approach to aesthetics, incorporating the latest medical technologies and treatments with natural and integrative therapies to help our patients look and feel their best. More about us

Wellness based modalities offered at Zia Skin + Wellness include IV treatments, and hormone replacement therapy using bioidentical hormones.

IV therapy is tailored to meet various client specific goals, such as improving nutritional deficiencies, overall hydration, energy management, and promotion of weight loss mechanisms. Other treatment aims with IV therapy include hangover relief and improved athletic performance recovery.

Bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT) aims to address hormonal imbalance as well as hormonal optimization to encourage our highest vibrancy. Symptoms of hormone imbalance can include fatigue, anxiety, depression, brain fog, hot flashes, weight again, and many other symptoms.
